Yu Zhang-SMU (张煜)

 

Yu Zhang received the M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in biomedical engineering from First Military Medical University, China, in 2000 and 2003, respectively.
He now is a Professor and the Vice Dean in School of Biomedical Engineering, Southern Medical University, China. His research interests include medical
image processing/analysis, survival analysis, and machine learning. In these areas, he has published over 80 scientific articles in refereed international
journals and conferences such as IEEE Trans. Neural Netw. Learn. Syst., IEEE Trans. Med. Imag., Med. Image Anal., NeuroImage, Bioinformatics, IEEE
J. Biomed. Health Informat., CVPR, MICCAI.

E-mail: yuzhang@smu.edu.cn
